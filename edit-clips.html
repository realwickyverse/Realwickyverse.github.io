Turn your raw stream recordings into YouTube videos and viral Shorts — fast, free, and without losing your mind.

01 — Free Editing Tools
WHICH APP TO USE
🎬
DaVinci Resolve
FREE
Pro-level editor used by Hollywood. Free version has everything a streamer needs. Best for long-form YouTube videos.
📱
CapCut
FREE
Best for Shorts. Auto-captions, trending effects, vertical format built-in. Use this for all your 60-second clips.
🖥
Clipchamp
FREE (built into Windows)
Already on your laptop. Great for quick trims and basic edits without installing anything. Perfect for beginners.
⚡
Kdenlive
FREE / Open Source
Lighter than DaVinci on your RAM. Good middle ground if your laptop struggles with heavy editing software.
💡 Recommendation
Start with Clipchamp (already installed) for quick cuts. Use CapCut on your iPhone for Shorts with captions. Graduate to DaVinci Resolve when you're ready for real editing.
02 — YouTube Video Workflow
LONG-FORM EDITING

📋 The 5-Step Clip Workflow

  • 1
    Find your momentsWatch your VOD at 2× speed. Mark timestamps of anything funny, impressive, or emotional. Look for: big kills, fails, reactions, funny chat moments.
  • 2
    Cut the fatRemove all dead air, loading screens, and slow moments. A 3-hour stream should become a 10–15 minute highlight video. Ruthlessly cut anything that isn't entertaining.
  • 3
    Add captionsUse CapCut's auto-caption or DaVinci's built-in speech recognition. Captions boost watch time by 20–30% — many people watch with sound off.
  • 4
    Add musicUse YouTube Audio Library (free, no copyright issues). Match energy to gameplay — intense music for action, chill music for downtime. Keep it under your voice volume.
  • 5
    Export settingsExport at 1080p, H.264, 60fps, ~20 Mbps for YouTube. DaVinci: use YouTube master preset. CapCut: tap Export → 1080p → 60fps.
03 — YouTube Shorts
SHORTS FORMULA
0–3 sec
The HookThe most exciting frame first. Start mid-action. "I can't believe this happened" or show the result before the buildup.
3–45 sec
The MomentShow the clip. Keep energy high. Add zoom-ins on key moments. Captions on everything.
45–55 sec
The ReactionYour face reaction, or a slow-mo replay, or funny sound effect. Reward the viewer for watching.
55–60 sec
The CTA"Follow for more" or "Watch the full stream" or just let it loop. Keep it short — don't beg.

📱 CapCut Shorts Checklist

  • → Crop to 9:16 vertical format first thing
  • → Add auto-captions — edit any mistakes
  • → Use a trending sound from CapCut's library
  • → Add zoom punch-in on the best moment
  • → Keep total length under 60 seconds
  • → First frame must show something exciting — not a black screen
04 — Saving Clips Fast
REPLAY BUFFER TRICK

⚡ Never Miss a Clip Again

Enable Replay Buffer in OBS so you don't have to record everything. Here's how:

  • → OBS → Settings → Output → check Enable Replay Buffer
  • → Set replay time to 60–120 seconds
  • → Set hotkey F12 = Save Replay
  • → After anything epic happens, hit F12 immediately
  • → OBS saves the last 60–120 seconds as a clean clip automatically
💡 Pro Move
Combine Replay Buffer with OBS Instant Replay plugin to see a 5-second preview of your saved clip right in OBS. Saves hours of scrubbing through footage.
