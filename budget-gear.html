03 — Accessories
SMALL UPGRADES, BIG IMPACT
01
❄️
Laptop Cooling Pad
~$20MUST HAVE

Your i5-11th Gen + RTX 3050 generates serious heat under load. A cooling pad keeps thermals in check so your laptop doesn't throttle mid-stream. Any brand works — just get one with 2+ fans.

✓ Prevents throttling ✓ Extends laptop life ✓ ~$15–20 anywhere
02
💡
Ring Light / Key Light
~$15BEST VALUE

Lighting makes your face cam look 10× better instantly. A small clip-on ring light for your monitor or a cheap desk lamp pointed at your face does wonders. Viewers notice good lighting more than camera quality.

✓ Biggest visual upgrade ✓ Clip-on options ✓ Under $20
03
🖱
External Mouse & Keyboard
~$25COMFORT

Using a laptop keyboard while gaming is rough. A basic wired gaming mouse + keyboard combo like the Redragon S101 improves gameplay and keeps your laptop cooler (less arm heat over keyboard vents).

✓ Better ergonomics ✓ Improves gameplay ✓ Bundle deals available
