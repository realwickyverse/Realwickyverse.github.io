03 — Audio Filters
PRO MIC CHAIN

Right-click your mic in OBS → Filters → Add these in this exact order:

  • 1
    Noise Suppression RNNoiseAI-powered background noise removal. Kills fan noise, AC, keyboard clicks. Always use RNNoise method, not Speex.
  • 2
    Noise Gate Close: -32dB / Open: -26dBMutes mic when you're not talking. Stops ambient noise bleeding through during silence.
  • 3
    Compressor Ratio 4:1 / Threshold -18dBEvens out your volume. Quiet moments get louder, loud moments get quieter. Sounds more professional.
  • 4
    Gain +3 to +6 dB if neededOnly add if your mic is too quiet after the compressor. Aim for peaks hitting -6dB on the OBS meter.
  • 5
    Limiter Threshold: -1dBLast safety net. Prevents any accidental clipping if you suddenly shout or the mic gets bumped.
⚠️ Audio Tip
Set your Desktop Audio to around -10dB in OBS mixer so game audio doesn't overpower your voice. Your voice should always be the loudest thing viewers hear.

💡 Replay Buffer
Enable Replay Buffer in OBS (Settings → Output → Replay Buffer). Set it to 60 seconds. Hit F12 after anything epic happens and OBS saves the last 60 seconds automatically — without recording everything.
