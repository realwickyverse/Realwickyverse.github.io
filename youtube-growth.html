01 — The Algorithm
HOW YOUTUBE FINDS YOU

🔍 Search vs Suggested

YouTube surfaces your content in two ways: Search (people look up a topic) and Suggested (YouTube recommends it). As a small channel, focus 100% on Search first — make content people are actively Googling, like "how to rank up in Valorant" or "best settings for Apex Legends RTX 3050". Suggested comes later once you have watch history data.

⏱ The 48-Hour Rule

YouTube judges every video in its first 48 hours. If people click your thumbnail and watch a good chunk of it, YouTube pushes it to more people. If they click away fast, it buries the video. This means your thumbnail and title are more important than anything else — they determine if people click at all.

📱 Shorts = Fastest Growth in 2025

YouTube Shorts are the single fastest way to grow a gaming channel right now. Clip your best 30–60 second moments from streams, add captions, post daily. The Shorts feed shows your content to non-subscribers — it's free advertising for your long-form content. Many channels hit 1K subs from Shorts alone within 30 days.

02 — Thumbnails & Titles
MAKE PEOPLE CLICK

✓ DO THIS

  • Big bold text (3 words max)
  • Your face with emotion
  • High contrast colors
  • Specific titles: "I hit Diamond after 200 hours"
  • Numbers in titles: "5 settings pros use"
  • Use Canva free templates

✗ AVOID THIS

  • Game logo as thumbnail
  • No text on thumbnail
  • Vague titles: "My stream highlights"
  • Dark, low contrast images
  • Clickbait you can't deliver
  • Episode numbers nobody knows
💡 Free Thumbnail Tool
Use Canva.com — search "YouTube Gaming Thumbnail" for free templates. Change the colors to match your brand. Takes 5 minutes per thumbnail once you have a template.
03 — Content Strategy
WHAT TO POST & WHEN

🗓 The 3-Type Content Mix

Rotate between these three content types every week:

  • Search content (40%) — "Best X settings", "How to improve at Y", "Top 5 Z tips". These get found forever.
  • Shorts (40%) — Daily clips from your streams. Fast to make, feeds the algorithm constantly.
  • Personality content (20%) — Your stream VODs, funny moments, personal milestones. Builds loyal fans.

🎯 Niche Down Hard

Don't be a "gaming channel" — be the best Valorant tips channel for beginners or the funniest Minecraft moments channel. YouTube's algorithm needs to know exactly who to show your content to. Pick one game or one format and go deep for at least 3 months before expanding.

📊 Use TubeBuddy Free

Install TubeBuddy (free Chrome extension). Before making any video, search your title idea on YouTube and check the competition score. Target keywords with High Search / Low Competition. This alone can 3× your views on the same content.

04 — Community
BUILD YOUR TRIBE

💬 Reply to Every Comment

For your first 6 months, reply to every single comment. This boosts engagement signals YouTube uses to rank videos, and turns casual viewers into loyal fans. Ask a question at the end of every video to spark comments.

🔴 Reddit & Discord

Post your guides (not just your channel) to relevant subreddits — r/NewTubers, r/letsplay, r/gaming, and game-specific subs. Don't spam — share genuinely helpful content. Create a Discord server on day one. Even 10 active Discord members will boost every stream you do.
